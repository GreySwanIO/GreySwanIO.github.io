Our Service Offerings

Grey Swan provides specialized security services focused on smart contracts and decentralized applications.

Smart Contract Audits

Comprehensive security audits of your Solidity, Rust, or other smart contracts. Our novel approach combines static analysis, expert review, and contract-specific property verification to identify vulnerabilities and ensure your code behaves as intended. This service is ideal for DeFi protocols, NFT projects, DAOs, and other blockchain-based applications.

Security Consulting & Advisory

Beyond audits, we offer expert security consulting to assist your team throughout the development lifecycle. This can include threat modeling, secure development training, and architectural reviews to help you build security in from the ground up.

Pre-Deployment Verification

A focused review before your mainnet launch, concentrating on critical vulnerabilities and ensuring that final code changes have not introduced new risks. This service provides an extra layer of confidence before going live.

Incident Response Support (Retainer)

For select clients, we offer incident response support to help navigate security breaches, analyze exploits, and provide guidance on mitigation and recovery.

All our services are tailored to meet the specific needs and complexity of your project. Contact us to discuss how we can help secure your blockchain innovations.
